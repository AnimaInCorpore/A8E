## 9.12 835 Hardware

## Controller

The 1030's controller is an 8048 microcontroller running at a clock rate of 4.032MHz. This is a variant of the 8048 with 1K of internal ROM and 64 bytes of internal RAM. There is no external ROM or RAM present.

A couple of lines are redundantly connected to the controller, including the SIO Data Out and SIO Command lines. The SIO Clock Out from the computer is also connected, though not used by the firmware.

## Known firmware

There is only one currently known version of firmware. The 1K internal ROM in the 8048 has a CRC32 of D6DD4F41.

## Modem hardware

Bell 103 compatible modulation and demodulation is performed by a TMS99532 single-chip Bell 103 compatible modem. It is a 'dumb' modem that has no AT command processing and communicates with the MCU using simple digital signals for serial data and control/sensing. Serial transmission over the modem is similar to the SIO bus, with a low start bit, eight data bits sent int LSB to MSB order, and a high stop bit.

The 835 also has the ability to conduct audio from the phone line to SIO Audio In so it can be heard through the TV speaker. It is enabled when bit 4 of P2 is pulled low. 59 There is no support for conducting audio in the other direction, from the computer to the phone line.
