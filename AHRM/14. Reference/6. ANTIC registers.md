## Register block

ANTIC occupies `$D400-$D40F`. The detailed register descriptions in this chapter cover:

- `DMACTL`
- `CHACTL`
- `DLISTL`, `DLISTH`
- `HSCROL`, `VSCROL`
- `PMBASE`, `CHBASE`
- `WSYNC`, `VCOUNT`
- `NMIEN`, `NMIST`, `NMIRES`
