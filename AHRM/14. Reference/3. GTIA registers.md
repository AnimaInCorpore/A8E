## Register block

GTIA occupies `$D000-$D01F`. The detailed register descriptions in this chapter cover:

- `HPOSP0-HPOSP3`, `M0PF-M3PF`
- `HPOSM0-HPOSM3`, `P0PF-P3PF`
- `SIZEP0-SIZEP3`, `M0PL-M3PL`
- `SIZEM`, `P0PL-P3PL`
- `GRAFP0-GRAFP3`, `TRIG0-TRIG3`
- `GRAFM`, `COLPM0-COLPM3`, `PAL`
- `COLPF0-COLPF3`, `COLBK`, `PRIOR`
- `VDELAY`, `GRACTL`, `HITCLR`, `CONSOL`
