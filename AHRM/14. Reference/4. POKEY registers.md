## Register block

POKEY occupies `$D200-$D20F`. The detailed register descriptions in this chapter cover:

- `AUDF1-AUDF4`, `POT0-POT7`
- `AUDC1-AUDC4`, `ALLPOT`
- `AUDCTL`, `STIMER`
- `KBCODE`, `SKRES`
- `RANDOM`, `POTGO`
- `SEROUT`, `SERIN`
- `IRQEN`, `IRQST`
- `SKCTL`, `SKSTAT`
