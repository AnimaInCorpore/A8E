## E.1 Introduction

The audio signal produced by an Atari computer results from a combination of digital and analog circuitry. The digital logic within POKEY is relatively well documented and understood, but the analog path less so. Some complex sound effects can only be realized through simulation of the non-linear analog path. In this appendix, a model is described for simulating the analog path as based on measurements taken from the actual hardware. Figure 27 shows a block overview of the analog model.
